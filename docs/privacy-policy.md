# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 26, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how spot. ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the spot. mobile application ("the App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and a unique user ID from Google. We do not receive your Google password or payment information.

### Saved Places
When you save a place, we store:
- The place name, address, coordinates (latitude/longitude), rating, price level, and category — sourced from Google Places
- Any personal note you attach to the place
- The date you visited (if you choose to add one)
- The date and time you saved the place

### Location
With your permission, we access your device's location to sort and filter saved places by distance. Location is used locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

### Shared URLs
When you share a link (e.g. from TikTok or Instagram) to spot., we extract the page title and description and send them to OpenAI's API to identify the place name. We do not store the raw URL or page content beyond the duration of that request.

### Usage Analytics
We use PostHog to collect anonymised usage data such as which features you use and how often (e.g. search performed, place saved). This data does not include the content of your notes or the specific places you save.

### Crash Reports
We use Sentry to collect crash and error reports to help us identify and fix bugs. These reports may include device type, OS version, and a stack trace, but do not include your personal content.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and sync your saved places across devices
- Enable place search via Google Places
- Extract place information from shared URLs via OpenAI
- Improve the App's reliability and performance
- Respond to support requests

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your saved places are stored:
- **Locally** on your device in an encrypted SQLite database
- **In the cloud** via Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on infrastructure in the United States

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through the App, your saved places and account information are scheduled for deletion within 30 days.
